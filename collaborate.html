Collaborate with Us

We're a lean, focused organisation — and we believe the best outcomes come from working alongside like-minded partners.

Why Collaborate
We can't do it all alone — and we don't want to

EcoDarpan is building a network of partners who share our mission of making global compliance accessible to Indian MSMEs. Whether you bring domain expertise, technology, market access, or capacity — there's likely a way to work together.

🏛️ Industry Associations & Chambers

We partner with trade bodies and export promotion councils to deliver compliance workshops, awareness programs, and advisory clinics for their member MSMEs. If your members are asking about EU ESG requirements, we can help you serve them.

🔬 Testing & Certification Labs

We work with accredited labs for REACH/SCIP chemical testing and certification. If you're a testing lab serving textile or leather exporters, we'd like to explore referral and integration partnerships.

💻 Technology Partners

We're building AI-driven compliance tools and are open to collaborating with teams working on supply chain traceability, ESG data platforms, or Digital Product Passport infrastructure.

📚 Academic & Research Institutions

We collaborate on research, training programs, and capacity-building initiatives. Our founding team has deep academic roots at IIT Kharagpur, IIM, and IMI — we value rigour and evidence-based approaches.

🌍 ESG & Sustainability Consultancies

If you're a consultancy with complementary strengths — say, carbon accounting, life cycle assessment, or specific industry verticals — we're open to co-delivery models where we each bring what we're best at.

🏦 Financial Institutions & Impact Funds

MSMEs need affordable financing to implement sustainability transitions. We're interested in connecting with institutions that offer green finance, sustainability-linked loans, or impact investment for the MSME sector.

How it Works
Simple, flexible partnerships

We keep things practical. Most collaborations start with a conversation about what each side brings and where the overlap is. We don't believe in rigid MoUs before proving value — let's do a pilot first and formalise what works.

📞

1. Initial Conversation

We discuss goals, capabilities, and where collaboration makes sense for both sides.

🧪

2. Pilot Engagement

We test the partnership with a small project or joint initiative before scaling.

🤝

3. Ongoing Partnership

If it works, we formalise the relationship and build together.
