About EcoDarpan

An execution-focused sustainability and compliance advisory firm, built from the ground up for Indian exporters.

Who We Are
From compliance to competitiveness

EcoDarpan Private Limited is an execution-focused sustainability and compliance advisory firm. We work with MSMEs, exporters, and small to mid-sized enterprises that are entering, scaling, or strengthening their presence in global markets.

Our clients are businesses that need to meet evolving compliance and ESG expectations from international buyers, regulators, and supply chains — and need a partner who will do the work alongside them, not just hand over a report.

We currently focus on textile and leather exporters navigating EU regulations like CSDDD, CSRD, EUDR, REACH, ESPR, and Textile EPR, with plans to expand into other compliance-heavy export sectors.

Vision

Enabling cost-effective, compliant, and globally resilient growth for Indian businesses.

Mission

To deliver practical, cost-effective, and integrity-led solutions that help businesses transition from compliance to competitiveness in global markets.
