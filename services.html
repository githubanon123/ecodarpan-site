Our Services

End-to-end, execution-driven solutions across compliance, ESG, and sustainability — organised into three practice areas.

🎯

Current focus: Textile & Leather MSMEs exporting to the EU

Our services are currently tailored for Indian textile and leather exporters navigating CSDDD, CSRD, EUDR, REACH, ESPR, and Textile EPR compliance. We're expanding to other sectors over time.

📋

Compliance & Regulatory

Navigate EU regulations, secure assurance, and stay audit-ready for your buyers.

Regulatory & Compliance Advisory

Hands-on support for CSDDD, CSRD, REACH, and EUDR compliance — from gap assessment through documentation, filings, and buyer questionnaire responses.

Assurance Services

Independent assurance of ESG and sustainability disclosures, giving EU buyers and auditors confidence in your reported data.

Chemical & Material Compliance (REACH/SCIP)

Testing coordination, SVHC documentation, and SCIP database submissions for textile and leather products entering the EU market.

🌱

ESG & Sustainability

Report, transition, and get recognised for your sustainability efforts.

ESG & Sustainability Reporting

Preparation of ESG reports aligned with global frameworks — GRI, BRSR, CDP — and structured to satisfy EU buyer requirements under CSRD value chain disclosures.

Sustainable Transition Advisory

Vendor matchmaking and identification of sustainable alternatives to your existing materials, dyes, chemicals, and processes — critical for ESPR readiness.

Sustainability Awards & Recognition

End-to-end support in identifying, applying for, and securing sustainability certifications and awards that strengthen your position with EU buyers.

📊

Strategic Advisory

Unlock incentives, measure impact, and make compliance financially viable.

Government Incentives & Opportunity Mapping

Identification of applicable GoI schemes, PLI incentives, and export benefits — with end-to-end application support to unlock them.

Cost-Benefit & Feasibility Analysis

Financial evaluation of sustainability investments — will switching to compliant dyes or sustainable leather tanning pay for itself through retained EU contracts?

Impact Assessment & Surveys

Measurement of outcomes through impact assessments and stakeholder surveys — turning your ESG effort into evidence for buyers and investors.

CSR Advisory

CSR need assessment, program design, implementation support, and impact evaluation — from strategy through reporting.
